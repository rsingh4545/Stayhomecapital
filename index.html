How We Protect Homes

StayHome Capital provides temporary mortgage payment relief for Canadian homeowners facing short-term financial hardship.

Simple Online Application

A short, secure eligibility form. No unnecessary paperwork. No delays.

Direct Bank Payments (6–24 Months)

We pay your mortgage directly to your lender for 6 to 24 months. Funds never go to the homeowner.

Ownership & Equity Protection

You remain the homeowner at all times. No monthly payments. Repayment occurs only when you sell or refinance.

Who This Program Is For

  • Homeowners facing temporary financial hardship
  • Those seeking to avoid foreclosure or forced sale
  • People who do not want high-interest loans
  • Homeowners with available equity

Important Transparency

  • We are not a bank
  • We are not a lender
  • We do not provide personal loans
  • We do not take ownership of your home
  • We register a legal lien to secure payments made
  • All terms are disclosed before approval
